Ideal job

A description (in your own words) of the position, and particularly what makes this position appealing to you.

Honestly, I have not decided which field I would like to go down yet. Perhaps I am still new to this industry and have not gained enough knowledge and information to recognise which part suits me better. To me, software programming is interesting and requires in-depth programming skills so that you can see what is going on and it is achieving. On the opposite, cyber security is quite different from software development which protects an organisation's networks and computer systems by identifying problems, evaluating security issues, assessing risk, and implementing solutions to address threats. it is exciting and challenging, and rewarding as well. Therefore, here I chose a position as a Software Engineer to be my ideal job or a goal I would like to achieve eventually. A Software Engineer is to design, develop and maintain software systems. What it appeals to me is that it provides a place for you to develop your creativity and it is a fulfilling and challenging career and rewarding salary.

Qualification: Must have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology and relevant majors. Working experience: 5 years experience in IT. Skills: The required programming skills for you to develop a new program that the company needs, problem-solving skills so you can test, debug, diagnose and correct errors and faults, maintain the program and system and provide advice, guidance and expertise.

And this is the reason I started studying for a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology.
