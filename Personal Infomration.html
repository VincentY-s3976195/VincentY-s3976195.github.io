Personal Information

I have joined a couple of bands playing different types of music such as Rock music, Punk, Nu Metal, Speed Metal, Alternative Rock etc. JOKER was a band I had been in for almost nine years and we had more than 500 lives around in Taiwan and several cities in China and released a few Eps and an album. In addition, I am also a recording drummer and a drums teacher.
